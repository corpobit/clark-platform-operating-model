# Clark Platform - Pricing and Engagement

## Overview

This document outlines the pricing model and engagement structure for Clark Platform services.

## Service Tiers

### Baseline Service

**Included**:
- Two core repositories (clark-platform-infra, clark-platform-control)
- Infrastructure provisioning and management
- Control plane installation and maintenance
- GitOps workflow setup and operation
- Basic monitoring and alerting
- Documentation and training
- Standard support

**Pricing Model**:
- Monthly subscription
- Based on infrastructure scale
- Predictable pricing

### Optional Services

**Available Add-Ons**:
- Secrets management
- Application GitOps
- CI/CD pipelines
- Enhanced observability
- Security and compliance tools

**Pricing Model**:
- One-time setup fee
- Monthly maintenance fee
- Usage-based (where applicable)

## Pricing Structure

### Baseline Pricing

**Factors**:
- Number of environments
- Infrastructure scale
- Support level required
- Contract duration

**Pricing Tiers**:
- **Starter**: Small teams, single environment
- **Professional**: Multiple environments, standard support
- **Enterprise**: Large scale, premium support

### Optional Services Pricing

**Setup Fees**:
- One-time implementation cost
- Based on complexity
- Includes training

**Monthly Fees**:
- Ongoing maintenance
- Support and updates
- Documentation updates

## Engagement Models

### Model 1: Full Managed Service

**Description**: Clark handles all platform operations

**Included**:
- All baseline services
- 24/7 monitoring
- Proactive management
- Incident response
- Regular reviews

**Best For**:
- Teams without platform expertise
- Focus on product development
- Need for reliability

### Model 2: Co-Managed Service

**Description**: Shared responsibility between Clark and customer

**Included**:
- Baseline services
- Business hours support
- Collaborative operations
- Knowledge sharing

**Best For**:
- Teams building platform capability
- Want to learn and participate
- Gradual transition

### Model 3: Consulting and Setup

**Description**: One-time setup with optional ongoing support

**Included**:
- Initial setup
- Training
- Documentation
- Optional support packages

**Best For**:
- Teams with existing capability
- Want to operate independently
- Need initial guidance
