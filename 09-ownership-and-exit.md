# Clark Platform - Ownership and Exit

## Overview

Customer ownership and the ability to exit are fundamental principles of the Clark Platform operating model. This document outlines ownership rights and exit procedures.

## Ownership Model

### Customer Ownership

Customers own and control:

- **Cloud Accounts**: Full ownership and control
- **Git Repositories**: Full ownership and control
- **Kubernetes Clusters**: Full ownership and control
- **Infrastructure**: Full ownership of provisioned resources
- **Data**: Complete data ownership
- **Code**: All code in repositories

### Clark Operation

Clark operates and maintains:

- **Infrastructure Provisioning**: Creates and manages infrastructure
- **Control Plane**: Installs and maintains Crossplane
- **GitOps Workflows**: Maintains GitOps processes
- **Documentation**: Creates and updates documentation
- **Support**: Provides operational support

### No Vendor Lock-In

- **No Proprietary Systems**: All open-source or standard tools
- **No Hidden Dependencies**: All dependencies documented
- **No Data Lock-In**: Customer owns all data
- **No Code Lock-In**: Customer owns all code

### Risk Reduction and Compliance

Full customer ownership and exit capability provide significant risk reduction and compliance benefits:

- **Regulatory Compliance**: Customer maintains full control over data and infrastructure, meeting strict compliance requirements
- **Audit Readiness**: Complete transparency and ownership simplify audit processes
- **Business Continuity**: Exit capability ensures business continuity regardless of vendor relationship
- **Operational Risk**: No single point of failure or dependency on vendor availability
- **Data Sovereignty**: Customer maintains complete data sovereignty and control

## Exit Capability

### Always Available

Customers can exit at any time:

- **No Lock-In Periods**: No contractual lock-in
- **No Penalties**: No exit penalties
- **Full Access**: Always have full access
- **Full Documentation**: All documentation provided

### Exit Process

#### 1. Notification

- **Customer Notifies**: Customer informs Clark of exit intent
- **Timeline Discussion**: Agree on transition timeline
- **Scope Definition**: Define what needs to be transferred

#### 2. Knowledge Transfer

- **Documentation Review**: Review all documentation
- **Training Sessions**: Train customer team
- **Handover Meetings**: Transfer knowledge
- **Q&A Sessions**: Answer questions

#### 3. Access Transfer

- **Full Access**: Customer already has full access
- **Operational Access**: Transfer any operational access
- **Credential Handover**: Transfer any shared credentials
- **Access Verification**: Verify customer has all access

#### 4. Code and Configuration Transfer

- **Repository Access**: Customer already owns repositories
- **Configuration Export**: Export all configurations
- **State Transfer**: Transfer Terraform state
- **Documentation**: Complete documentation package

#### 5. Support Transition

- **Transition Period**: Optional transition support
- **Support Handoff**: Hand off to customer team
- **Final Review**: Final review and sign-off

### Exit Timeline

**Standard Exit**:
- **Week 1**: Notification and planning
- **Week 2-3**: Knowledge transfer
- **Week 4**: Access transfer and handoff

**Accelerated Exit**:
- **Week 1**: Notification and transfer
- **Week 2**: Handoff

*Note: Timeline depends on complexity and customer readiness*

## Exit Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Internal Team Ready

- **Situation**: Customer has internal team ready
- **Process**: Standard exit process
- **Timeline**: 2-4 weeks
- **Support**: Minimal transition support needed

### Scenario 2: Hiring Team

- **Situation**: Customer hiring team
- **Process**: Extended transition period
- **Timeline**: 2-3 months
- **Support**: Extended transition support

### Scenario 3: Alternative Provider

- **Situation**: Customer switching providers
- **Process**: Standard exit + provider handoff
- **Timeline**: 3-4 weeks
- **Support**: Provider-specific handoff

### Scenario 4: Simplification

- **Situation**: Customer simplifying infrastructure
- **Process**: Standard exit + simplification support
- **Timeline**: 2-4 weeks
- **Support**: Simplification guidance
